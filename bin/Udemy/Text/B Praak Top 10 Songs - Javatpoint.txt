            B Praak Top 10 Songs  Music makes one's mood calm and peaceful, and it is one of the favorite pass times for all people. Whenever someone feels bored or stressed, the melody of music makes them stress-free and happy. There is a power in music that can make one's day and work energetic and enjoyable. What is a song? Songs are a musical composition which is composed by songwriters, composers, and lyricists for professional singers. Music is also a bright career option. There are many singers and whose songs are very popular and liked by the people. B Praak is also one of the popular singers whose songs are very popular among people. B Praak life & works B Praak original name is Pratik Bachchan. On the stage, he is known as B Praak. He is an Indian Punjabi singer, music composer, and producer. He was born on 7th February 1986 in Chandigarh. Praak started his career as a music director in 2009. But that time, he did not get any recognition. In 2012 after meeting Janni, a lyricist, they worked together, and next year (2013), their first song, "Soch," was released. The song is sung by Hardy Sandhu. After the success of Soch, Praak never looked back, and he composed and produced many songs sung by several singers like; Hardy Sandhu, Diljit Dosanjh, Amrinder Gill, etc. In 2018 he debuted as a singer with the song Mann Bharrya. He produced music in the film Qismat. In 2019 Praak made his debut in the Hindi film Kesari, in which he sang the song Teri Mitti. Praak gave a number of hit songs from 2009 till the present. The top 10 songs of B Praak are as follows: 1. Ranjha:  Ranjha is the latest song of the movie Shershaah. The singer of the song is B Praak and Jasleen Royal. The lyrics of the song are written by Anvita Dutt, and Akshay Raheja gave the music. The video of the song is directed by Vishnuvardhan, and the casts of the song are Sidharth Malhotra and Kiara Advani. The song was released by Sony Music India in 2021. 2. Baarish ki jaaye:  It is the latest song from the album Baarish Ki Jaye. B Praak is the singer of this song, and the casts of this song are Nawazuddin Siddiqui and Sunanda Sharma. The music composer is B Praak, and the lyric of the song is written by Janni. Janni and Arvindr are the music producer, and Arvindr Khaira is the director of the music. The song was released by Desi Melodies on 24th March 2021. 3. Filhaal2 Mohabbat:  It is also the latest romantic song of the album Filhaal2 Mohabbat. The song is sung by B Praak. Janni wrote the lyrics of the song, and music is given by B Praak. The cast of the song is Akshay Kumar, Nupur Sanon, and Ammy Virk. The song is directed by Arvindr Khaira and released by Desi Melodies in 2021. 4. Mann bharryaa 2.0:  This is also the latest and heart-touching song from the movie Shershaah. The lyrics of the song are written by Janni, and the music is given by B Praak. The singer of the song is also B Praak, and the video of the song is directed by Vishuvardhan. The cast of the song is Sidhart Malhotra and Kiara Advani. The song was released by Sony Music India in 2021. 5. O Saki Saki:  This song is from the movie Batla House. The singer of the song is Neha Kakkar, Tulsi Kumar and B Praak. The lyrics of the song are written by Tanishk Bagchi and Dev Kohli, and music is given by Tanishk Bagchi and Vishal-Shekhar. The cast of the song is Nora Fatehi and John Abraham. The song was released in 2019 by T-Series. 6. Jannat:  This song is from the Sufna movie. It is the latest Punjabi song. The singer of the song is B Praak, and the lyrics of the song are written by Janni. B Praak also gave the music of the song, and it is directed by Jagdeep Sidhu. The cast of the song is Ammy Virk and Tania. The song was released by speed records in 2020. 7. Dil Tod Ke:  This song is from the album Dil Tod Ke, sung by B Praak, and the lyrics of the song are penned down by Manoj Muntashir. Rochak Kholi gave the music of the song, and the video is directed by Ashish Panda. The star casts of the song are Abhishek Singh, Kashish Vohra, and Jubin Shah. T-Series released the music video on 15th July 2020. 8. Ik Varri Hor Soch lae:   This song is from Qismat & other hits album. B Praak composed and produced the music of this Punjabi song. The song was written by Jaani and directed by Anurag Singh. The singer of the song is Harish Verma, and the song was released by Speed Records in 2018. 9. Filhall:  It is a Punjabi song of the album Filhall sung by B Praak. B Praak is also the music director of this song. The lyrics of the song are penned down by Janni, and the video is directed by Arvind Khaira. The star casts of this song are Akshay Kumar and Nupur Sanon. The song was released by Desi Melodies in 2019. This song also made the record of 100 million views on YouTube. 10. Teri Mitti:  This is a patriotic song from the movie Kesari. B Praak is the singer of this song, and Manoj Muntashir wrote the lyrics of this song. The music is composed by Arko. The star cast of the song is Akshay Kumar and Parineeti Chopra, and the song was released by the Zee Music Company in 2019.
