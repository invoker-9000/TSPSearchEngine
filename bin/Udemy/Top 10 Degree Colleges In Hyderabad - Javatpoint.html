Top 10 Degree Colleges In Hyderabad

Every Individual gets bothered about their career once in life once they are done with their 12th. And taking the academic at another level needs some precautions. In this article, we will discuss and try to end it with the proper guidance. In this particular article, we will discuss the top degree college available in Hyderabad. Here Degree College means all those colleges that provide specializations in either undergraduate program or postgraduate degree programs.

How Bachelor Degree Is Important?

The primary factor is that a bachelor's degree has become so famous because it opens up many opportunities for you to build a successful career ahead. The costs differ from institution to institution depending on the courses, colleges, and services they offer. Some universities provide direct admissions, while others require students to take entrance tests. So let's start the discussion and take a look at the finest degree colleges in Hyderabad.

Top 10 Degree College In Hyderabad

1. Nizam College

Nizam College is among South India's oldest educational institutions. Nizam Institution, a constituent college of Osmania University, and was founded in 1887. This college provides UG and PG programmes in the arts, commerce, science, and social science areas. Courses are offered in several distinct streams. For admission, the institute follows the norms and procedures of the government's centralized seat assignment system. Aspirants to enrol in undergraduate programmes in Nizam must get it through the online enrollment process on the DOST Telangana site.

Address LB Stadium Rd, Gun Foundry, Basheer Bagh, Hyderabad, Telangana 500001
Course Offered Physical Sciences, Biological Sciences, B.Com, B.A. Degree - Telugu Medium, B.A. Degree - English Medium, Bachelor Of Bussiness Administration (B.B.A.), Bachelor Of Computer Applications (B.C.A.) and Post Graduates Courses
Official Website https://www.nizamcollege.ac.in/

2. A V College of Arts, Science & Commerce

The A. V. Education Association is among the oldest and most innovative educational organizations in Hyderabad, the former Nizam's State. This organization was founded in 1944 to provide education and opportunities to primitive categories of the area that were denied access to education during the Nizam's reign.

This college provides almost all the graduate and postgraduate programs and can be a better pick.

Address Gagan Mahal Rd, Gagan Mahal, Domalguda, Himayatnagar, Hyderabad, Telangana 500029
Course Offered B.Sc., B.Com, M.Sc. MBA, B.A.,(MCA), M.A., Master Of Commerce M.Com
Official Website http://www.avcollege.in/

3. Francis College for Women

St Francis College for Women is a well-known name in the field of delivering superior education for women in the areas of commerce and science. It is connected with Osmania University, UGC-approved, and AICTE-recognized. The institute was founded in 1959 and had a reputation for producing top-notch individuals in the disciplines of business and science. Its goal is to provide a sound knowledge base and the confidence needed to manage various scenarios in the future.

Address 6, Street Number 6, Uma Nagar, Begumpet, Hyderabad, Telangana 500016
Course Offered B.Sc., B.Com, B.A., M.sc, Master Of Arts (M.A.), Bachelor of Management Studies (BMS)
Official Website https://www.sfc.ac.in/

4. Loyola Academy Degree & PG College

This college was founded in 1975 and is dedicated to rigorous standards and quality, and its infrastructure is impressive. Furthermore, the university tries to make its learners globally competitive, making them proud nation representatives. Business Administration/Management, Computer Sciences, Agri-Science and Technology, Commerce and Information Technology, Engineering, and a few others streams are also taught at the college.

Address Alwal Rd, Manjeera Colony, Spring Fields Colony, Jeedimetla, Hyderabad, Telangana 500015
Course Offered B.Sc., B.Com, M.Sc. MBA, B.A., MCA, Master Of Arts (M.A.), Master Of Commerce (M.Com)
Official Website https://www.loyolaacademyugpg.ac.in/

5. Ann's College for Women

In 1983, St. Ann's College for Women opened its doors with two undergraduate degrees and 106 students. It has since grown into an exceptional institution, establishing its ideal "Light of Life." The college's attraction transcends social, economical, cultural, and language barriers.

As a missionary institution, the goal is to cultivate a vibrant campus culture that emphasizes the importance of an ethical element, transparency, and sociological phenomena, which, along with academic excellence and skill growth, make up the entire educational experience.

Address 12-2-823/A/45, St Ann's Rd, Santosh Nagar, Mehdipatnam, Hyderabad, Telangana 500028
Course Offered B.Sc., B.Com, Master Of Science (M.Sc). Master Of Business Administration (MBA), B.A., (MCA), (M.A.), Master Of Commerce (M.Com)
Official Website http://www.stannscollegehyd.com/

6. Bhavan's New Science College

In the Indian State of Telangana, Bhavan's New Science College is located in Hyderabad. It BNSO is a private institution that was founded in 1956. Bhavan's New Science College provides 15 courses in four streams: science, information technology, the arts, commerce, and banking. BSc, BA, B.Com, BCA, and MSc are some of the most popular courses offered at Bhavan's New Science College. Bhavan's New Science College is a leader in research and innovation and has a strong teaching pedagogy. Bhavan's New Science College places a strong emphasis on activities outside of the classroom, as evidenced by its infrastructure, extracurricular activities, and national and international relationships.

Address King Koti Road, opposite Shalimar Function Hall, Hyderabad, Telangana 500029
Course Offered Bachelor Of Science (B.Sc.), Bachelor Of Arts (BA), Bachelor Of Commerce (B.Com), Master Of Science (M.Sc)., Master Of Science (M.Sc.),
Official Website https://www.bhavans.info/college/college3.asp

7. Indian Institute of Management and Commerce

The Indian Institute of Management and Commerce (IIMC) is a well-known institute in Hyderabad's Khairatabad. It was founded in 1973 and is accredited by NAAC with a grade of 'B'. Various magazines have ranked the Institute, including The Week, India Today, Education World, and Republic World. It has established itself as a prominent institution in the country's field of commerce education.

Full-time undergraduate and postgraduate programmes in Commerce, Science, and Business are available at IIMC. For both UG and PG programmes, enrollment to the Institute is merit-based as well as entrance-based.

Address 6-1-91, Central Secretariat, Khairtabad, Hyderabad, Telangana 500004
Course Offered Bachelor Of Science (B.Sc.), B.Com, BBA, Bachelor Of Art (B.A.),
Official Website http://www.iimchyderabad.com/

8. Badruka College of Commerce & Arts

Badruka College of Commerce and Arts (BCCA) is a historically significant college in Hyderabad, Telangana, associated with Osmania University and offers B.Com and BBA programmes.

The Seth Gopikishan Educational and Charitable Trust was initially in charge of the college, formed in 1950. Seth Ghasiram Gopikishan Badruka Educational Society was founded in 1966 and incorporated under the Public Societies Registration Act. The Society's educational initiatives have grown throughout time. The Society currently manages nine institutes of higher learning, including Information Technology, Economics, Commerce, Management, Chartered Accountancy, Vocational Courses, and Music.

Address Kachiguda Station Rd, Rahamath Bagh, Mahalaxmi Nilayam, Kachiguda, Hyderabad, Telangana 500027
Course Offered B.Sc., B.Com, Master Of Science (M.Sc). Master Of Business Administration (MBA), B.A., MCA, Master Of Arts (M.A.), Master Of Commerce (M.Com)
Official Website http://www.badruka.com/

9. City College

It is an autonomous college and oldest historic building in Hyderabad that offers degree courses. The National Assessment and Accreditation Council have re-accredited Government City College with a 'B++' mark. Since 2004, the University Grants Commission, Osmania University, and the Telangana State Government have granted it autonomy. Since the academic year 2014-15, the college has used CBCS.

Address City College Rd, High Court, Ghansi Bazaar, Hyderabad, Telangana 500002
Course Offered B.Sc., B.Com, Master Of Science (M.Sc), B.A
Official Website https://gdcts.cgg.gov.in/charminar.edu

10. Aurora's Degree and PG College

Over 2 decades, Aurora's Degree College has created a place for itself. Its constant commitment to providing high-quality education through participative teaching approaches and pedagogical tools has aided in developing students' personalities.

Aurora is more than a college, and it is a commiting to developing a new generation of brilliant and capable persons who will be the caretakers of the future. Aurora's Degree College, ranked among the top fifty colleges in the country, has a strong reputation for academic excellence and a holistic approach to student development.

Address 1-2-168/2/A, Street No.12, Chikkadpally, Hyderabad, Telangana-500 020.
Course Offered B.Sc., B.Com, Master Of Science (M.Sc). Master Of Business Administration (MBA), B.A., MCA, Master Of Arts (M.A.), Master Of Commerce (M.Com)
Official Website https://www.adc.edu.in/
