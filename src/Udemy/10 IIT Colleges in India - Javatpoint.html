10 IIT Colleges in India

IITs (Indian Institute of Technology) are the most reputable and finest technical institution in India. IITs are not just an institution, it is a dream of every JEE aspirant.

Admission to these technical colleges attracts students from all around the country. Each year, a large number of students, prepare throughout the year and give the entrance exam for taking a seat in IIT. IIT offers both the program; Undergraduate (B. Tech) and Postgraduate (M. Tech).

There are currently 23 IITs in India, all of which are administered by the Institute of Technology Act, 1961, which establishes a common IT council. Though there is no doubting that IIT offers students a fantastic career path and job prospects.

Total IITs in India

There are a total of 23 IITs in India, out of which seven IITs are very old and the remaining 16 IITs have been established after 2004.

Top Alumni of IIT

For numerous understudies, fair being conceded to IIT may be a dream come genuine. For a few, it is as it were the starting of a journey that will lead to unfathomable accomplishment. Fruitful IIT graduated class has propelled eras of understudies, from top-tier CEOs to well-known researchers, from young business visionaries to unique entertainers.

It is the most prestigious institution that gives the nation great leaders, businessman, and tech-savvy professionals. Here's we are mentioning a few of the top IITians of India:

  • Sundar Pichai, CEO of Google
  • Raghuram Rajan, Economist and former Governor of Reserve Bank of India,
  • Deepindar Goyal, Founder of Zomato
  • R. Narayan Murthy, founder of Infosys
  • Arvind Kejriwal, CM of Delhi
  • Manohar Parrikar, former Defence Minister of India

List of Top 10 IIT Colleges in India

If you are a JEE aspirant and facing confusion to choose the right IIT for you, then don't worry! We are helping you to figure out the right IIT. So let's deep dive into the top 10 IIT:

IIT Madras (Chennai)

IIT Madras has founded in 1959. IIT Madras has given great alumni, who are serving the nation by different professions. IIT Madras has world-class facilities for becoming engineers.

The institution has scored 85% by NIRF ( National Institutional Ranking Framework) in 2020.

Facilities:

The campus of IIT Madras is less with the central library AEE Central Workshop, Central Electronics Centre, Central glass blowing section, etc. All these facilities are like a cherry on cake.

  • IIT Madras hazard 20 teraflops supercomputers. It has a shopping Centre, Gym, swimming pool, food courts, cricket, hockey, football, and badminton stadiums.
  • The campus has residential accommodations for students and faculties. There are three banks, Canara Bank, ICICI, and SBI in the campus.
  • The institution has also three temples: Ganapathi temple, Durga Peliamman, and Jalakanteshwara.

IIT Bombay (Mumbai)

IIT Bombay was established in 1958. It is situated in Powai, Mumbai. IIT Bombay was the first IIT in India. It was set up with the help of foreigners. The reserves from UNESCO came as Roubles from the at that point Soviet Union.

IIT Bombay is the very first center of attraction for all the JEE aspirants. You can understand this with this fact; last year, 75% of qualifying candidates among the top 100 students choose IIT Bombay.

Although it is known for engineering, IIT Bombay is also known for its courses in philosophy and sociology. So besides academics, Bombay has a vibrant culture.

Facilities:

  • The campus has a Central Library, Staff Club, Hospital, Guest House, Women's Cell, Kendriya Vidyalaya, Estate Office, Travel Desk etc.
  • IIT Bombay is also famous for its Techfest, Annual Science and Technology Festival. These programs are held every year December. The people from the different cities and institutes have come to Bombay with their science project or innovation.
  • The annual cultural festival of IIT Bombay is known as the Mood Indigo. It is the largest cultural festival in Asia.

IIT Roorkee (Uttarakhand)

University of Roorkee and Thomason College of Civil Engineering was the former name of IIT Roorkee. It has established in the year 1847. It is located on the spiritual land of Uttarakhand which makes it more special.

There are total of 21 academic departments that offer 11 Undergraduate, 1 Integrated Dual Degree, 61 Postgraduate, and a variety of Doctoral programs.

Facilities:

  • There are post offices, Banks, and 24X7 functionality ATMs. Also, all the sports facilities are available on the campus.
  • The campus has beautiful located hangout places.
  • There is also a Railway Reservation Counter inside the campus.

IIT Kharagpur

IIT Kharagpur oldest and most prestigious one of the IIT in India. It is founded in 1951. The campus of IIT Kharagpur has spread over 2100 acres.

There are all the facilities of world-class University.

Facilities:

  • The campus has provided with food courts, grocery stores, market, bank and ATMs.
  • The students have allowed having a personal mode of transport inside the campus.
  • The campus has also provided guest house and canteen for the visitors.

IIT Kanpur (Uttar Pradesh)

IIT Kanpur was founded in 1959. This institute has established under Kanpur Indo-American Programme.

IIT Kanpur's library contains about 3 lakh volumes, and periodicals in its collection, with nearly 4500 new books and journals added each year.

IIT Kanpur is a public research institute that works on a variety of social and public-benefit initiatives. IIT Kanpur administered the JEE Advanced 2018 exams.

Facilities:

  • IIT Kanpur has spent 71 crores of its annual expenditure on the institution.
  • The placement percentage of the institution is 95.8%. The average salary package of its students is around 15 Lacks.
  • The overall NIRF score of IIT Kanpur is 82.18%.
  • The institute has less with several laboratories such as Microwave Circuits Laboratory, RFID Laboratory, Nanotechnology laboratory, Central Library, R&D Automation, and so on.

IIT Delhi

IIT Delhi has founded in Hauz Khas, Delhi, in 1961. The College of Engineering and Technology was the original name of the institute, but it was changed to IIT in 1963.

The institute is located in the middle of the Nation's Heart, the national capital. It is surrounded by landmarks such as the Qutub Minar and the Lotus Temple.

The hostels on the IIT Delhi campus are named after Indian mountain ranges, and the university is ranked fourth among the most prestigious universities in the country.

Facilities:

  • The institution has Central Research Facility, Central Workshop, Nanoscale Research Facility, and DAILAB.
  • There are two banks, SBI and Canara Bank inside the IIT Delhi campus.
  • The campus has its hospital that opens 24X7. IIT Delhi also provides medical insurance up to 5 Lacks.
  • The campus has so many food courts as well.

IIT BHU Varanasi

Students come from several corners of the world, and to take admission in IIT BHU. IIT BHU is one of the oldest technical institutions in India. It is founded in 1991.

There are more than 5000 scholars, who receive a world-class education at IIT BHU. Impressively, 97% of undergraduate students of IIT BHU used to get the average starting package of around 13 lakh.

The annual expenditure of IIT BHU is around for 3 crores. The teaching-learning and resources are pretty advanced in this IIT. IIT BHU is also known for excellent research work.

Faculties:

  • IIT BHU has overall scored 62.5% by NIRF.
  • The campus has spread over 1400 Acres. The institute has provided an excellent 24X7 internet facility to the campus.
  • IIT BHU has one of the largest library in India.
  • All kinds of sports facilities are available on the campus, basketball, tennis, badminton, swimming, etc.
  • There is another facility of IIT BHU. The institute has allowed students to have their own bikes or scooters.

IIT Indore ( Madhya Pradesh)

IIT Indore was established by the Indian government in the year 2009.

IIT Indore has a total student intake is more than 1000 students. 85% of students of IIT BHU got an average salary of around 13 lakh. IIT Indore has spent 31 crores as the annual capital expenditure. IIT Indore has code 79.8% by NIRF.

Facilities:

  • The campus has provided great facilities. The campus is less with vegetable shop, canteen, café, laundry services, Amul parlor, and salon.
  • There is the post office, and Canara Bank inside the campus.
  • The campus hosts 3 ATMs. All these ATMs function 24X7.

IIT Guwahati (Assam)

IIT Guwahati, the sixth oldest IIT in India. It was founded in 1993 in Guwahati, Assam. It is located at the bank of the Brahmaputra River. Its location view will mesmerize you.

The institute has quickly risen to become one of India's top 10 IITs. The NIRF score of IIT Guwahati is 69.7%.

Facilities:

  • IIT Guwahati has 11 academic departments and 10 research centres.
  • The campus has grocery stores, tailor shops, café and restaurants, and salons.
  • The campus has beautiful greenery.

IIT Hyderabad (Telangana)

IIT Hyderabad has established in 2008. It is among the eighth young Indian Institute of Technology. It has a total of more than 2600 students. 1,155 belong to the undergraduate program, 635 belong to the postgraduate and 1085 students are pursuing Ph.D.

The overall NIRF score of the institution is 59.53%.

Facilities:

  • The campus has spread over 526 Acres.
  • The campus has provided a central library, transport facility, common dining, café, grocery store, bank, and ATMs.
  • IIT Hyderabad has more than 100 research laboratories.
