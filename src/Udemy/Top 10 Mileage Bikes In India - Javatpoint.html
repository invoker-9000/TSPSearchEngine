Top 10 Mileage Bikes In India

Are you also a fan of bikes, but do you often worry about fuel efficiency?

But at the same time, on the other hand, are you concerned about your preferences.?

Then we have got a remedy, especially for you.

The listing of the 'top 10 mileage bikes in India' has been compiled for you using online search data from more than 50 lakh consumers in India. Mileage is a crucial element to take into consideration while purchasing a motorcycle.

To make the optimal decision, other factors such as engine power, maintenance costs, and so on must be considered.

We present you information about the greatest mileage bikes' ex-showroom price, colours, versions, monthly units sold, popularity, and launch date to help you choose the most suitable one. The blog is a perfect compilation, and it is recommended that you look through our list of the top 10 mileage bikes in India if you want to get the greatest mileage bikes. The following list was compiled keeping in mind all the factors.

With the escalating cost of petroleum and diesel, most Indian bike riders are concerned about mileage and fuel efficiency. Regular commuters, in particular, should evaluate how they might reduce their everyday travel expenses while riding their dependable bikes. Keeping this difficulty in consideration for prospective bike buyers, here is a rundown of some of India's greatest mileage bikes.

The bikes on our list were ranked based on their mileage. Below, you can also discover information about their engine displacement and ex-showroom pricing (Delhi).

List of Top 10 Mileage Bikes In India

1. The TVS Sport (Mileage: 95kmpl)

Number one on the list of 10 mileage bikes in India is the TVS sport, and it is said to have an astounding mileage of 95kmpl.

Fuel efficiency is a primary issue for any commuter motorbike buyer. If you want to save money on your regular commute on a bike, the TVS Sport should be your first choice. This bike, which is exceptionally dependable. This was inducted into the Asia Book of Records in 2019 as one of the most fuel-efficient bikes on the Golden Quadrilateral.

Aside from its mileage, other prominent characteristics of this bike include a single-cylinder, air-cooled engine capable of generating 7.4bhp and 7.3Nm of torque and 100cc engine capacity.

TVS Motors' sleek entry-level commuter vehicle. The Sport is specifically developed for riders who seek beautiful bikes with high fuel efficiency at a low price. Some of its characteristics include a CVTI engine that gives excellent mileage, attractive five-spoke alloy wheels, a dynamic multi-reflector headlamp, an electric engine beginning, appealing tail lamps, and an electrical indicator for low fuel identification.

Whereas in terms of cost it is priced at approx Rs.39,900.

2. Bajaj Platina 100 (Mileage: 90kmpl)

Number Two of the list of 10 mileage bikes in India is the Bajaj Platina 100. It is said to have an astounding mileage of 90kmpl.

Fuel efficiency is no more a concern of worry for commuters and motorbike buyers with this beauty.

The Bajaj Platina 100, which comes in two bold colours, black and red, is yet another incredibly fuel-efficient bike. It offers a comfortable riding experience with cutting-edge suspension, and this bike also comes with a 4-speed manual transmission.

When it comes to engine statistics, this two-wheeler can produce an astonishing 7.9bhp. The lack of a front brake disc, on the other hand, can be considered a disadvantage for this bike. The amazing bike has a 102cc engine capacity.

The Bajaj Platina is a 100cc bike produced by Bajaj Auto. Within 8 months of its release, the group had sold 500,000 bikes. There have been many upgrades, modifications in this bike since it was launched. Many features have been upgrades, and as of the current model, it retains the very same characteristics as the prior generation. However, the graphics have been revised to imitate its more mighty counterpart, the CT110, with the notable exception of the apparent lack of bellows encompassing the front suspension and fuel tank guards, which are conventional on the CT110.

Last but not the least, round headlamps, rear headlights similar to the CT 100B, a much more rugged appearance, and an extra luggage rack for off-road riding are distinctive elements.

The cost of this beauty is reasonably priced at Rs.40,897, making it one of the top 10 mileage bikes in India at such a cost and features.

3. Bajaj CT100 (Mileage: 89kmpl)

Number Three of the list of 10 mileage bikes in India is the Bajaj CT100. It is said to have a spectacular mileage of 89kmpl.

Fuel efficiency is no more a concern of worry for commuters and motorbike buyers with this beauty.

The CT 100 is one of the best mileage bikes in India, and it also has a nice ride. This motorcycle has a dependable air-cooled, single-cylinder engine, and it has a maximum output of 8.1bhp and a peak torque of 8.05Nm. Buyers wanting to purchase the bike can select from four colour choices: blue, red, or colour combinations like red with black and blue with black.

At the 2020 Droom Awards, this bike was named Jury's Choice Pre-Owned Commuter Bike of the Year.

The bike is furnished with a 99.28cc engine capacity, whereas the cost of the bike is reasonably placed at Rs.33,402.

4. TVS Star City Plus (Mileage: 86kmpl)

Number fourth on the list of 10 mileage bikes in India is the TVS Star City Plus.TVS has a good reputation in the market with fuel efficiency bikes, and this is one of the bikes that TVS that is said to have a spectacular mileage of 86kmpl.

It is only reasonable to evaluate this TVS model when searching for the most fuel-efficient bike in India. The Star City Plus is a famous commuter bike with excellent fuel efficiency. However, it also excels in other areas.

Consumers are drawn to this car because of its long comfy seats, enormously potent engine efficiency (with a maximal power of 8.30bhp), and other characteristics.

The engine capacity of the said bike is 109.7cc, whereas the cost of the same is Rs.53,047.

5. Honda Dream Yuga (Mileage: 84kmpl)

On number five in the list of 10 mileage bikes in India is the Honda Dream Yuga. This bike has a good reputation in the market in the category of fuel efficiency bikes, and this is one of the bikes that have an amazing mileage of 84kmpl.

When it concerns to mileage, Honda's motorcycles could never be much behind. Honda's dream Yuga, for example, is suitable for most Indian roads and has excellent fuel efficiency. It, too, does have a 4-speed transmission and a single-cylinder engine.

Another characteristic of this variant is the HET technology, which enhances mileage efficiency even further. The Indian Design Council awarded this bike the India Design Mark in 2013.

The bike has a 109.19cc engine capacity, and the cost of the bike is approximately Rs.55,233.

6. Yamaha Saluto RX (Mileage: 82kmpl)

Next on the list of 10 mileage bikes in India is the Yamaha Saluto RX. Yamaha is a renowned brand for bikes that have amazing performance and fuel efficiency bikes, and this is one of the bikes that have an amazing mileage of 82kmpl.

The Saluto RX from Japanese automaker Yamaha comes next on this ranking of the greatest mileage bikes in India.

Aside from its excellent fuel economy, the bike also has a strong pick-up and acceleration. At 4500rpm, the engine can produce a torque of roughly 8.50Nm. This motorcycle is now available in six vibrant colours.

The bike has an engine capacity of 110cc, and the cost of the bike is Rs.60,880.

7. Hero Splendor Plus (Mileage: 81kmpl)

On number seven in the list of 10 mileage bikes in India is the Hero Splendor Plus. This bike has a good reputation in the market in the category of fuel efficiency bikes, and this is one of the bikes that have an amazing mileage of 81kmpl.

Hero Motocorp's amazing hard-core commuter bike from the Splendor plus is another exemplar of a bike with remarkable mileage figures. This, along with many other characteristics, has propelled this bike to the top of the budget sector. Customers can customise this commuting vehicle with alloy wheels and self-start functions. Hero currently sells this two-wheeler in seven appealing colours.

97.2cc engine capacity marks another characteristic of the bike, and the cost of the same is Rs.53,790.

8. TVS Radeon (Mileage: 70kmpl)

Next on the list is TVS Radeon. This bike stands at number 8 on the list of 10 mileage bikes in India.

This bike is another bike of TVS that has a good reputation in the market in the category of fuel efficiency bikes, and this is one of the bikes that have a comparatively good mileage of 70kmpl.

Another TVS contender on our list, the Radeon, provides excellent mileage while being less expensive than the Star City Plus. This bike offers a lot of characteristics, like drum brakes, a 4-speed transmission, tubeless tyres, and self-start, in addition to its excellent mileage. It appears to be fairly enticing, emphasizing simplicity while preserving style. This bike has won numerous honours and is available in four colour options to buyers.

The bike has a 109.7cc engine capacity, and the cost is placed at Rs.50,820.

9. TVS Victor (Mileage: 70kmpl)

Yet another beauty on the list is TVS Victor. This bike stands at number 9 on the list of 10 mileage bikes in India.

This bike is another bike of TVS that is renowned for being a good fuel efficiency bike, and this is one of the bikes that have a comparatively good mileage of 70kmpl.

Victor is a 110cc motorcycle with excellent handling and stunning performance. However, its standout features are its finest mileage and outstanding styling. It is full of incredible feats, with a maximum output figure of roughly 9.6bhp at 7500rpm. A digital speedometer and comfy chairs are two further things you may expect from this bike.

109.70cc is the engine capacity, and the cost of the same is Rs.55,022.

10. Bajaj Discover 110 (Mileage: 69kmpl)

The last entry in the list is Bajaj Discover 110. This bike stands at number 10 in the list of 10 mileage bikes in India. This bike has a good look and good performance with good fuel efficiency, and this is one of the bikes that have an amazing mileage of 69 kmpl.

The Discover 110, another amazing Bajaj motorcycle, is the final item on this list of the finest mileage bikes in India. Aside from the mileage, notable characteristics include i3s technology for a better riding experience. This bike has a power rating of 8.6bhp at 7000rpm and a maximal torque of 9.81Nm at 5,000rpm. Grey Metallic, Red Metallic, Black, White, Blue Metallic, and Brown Metallic are this vehicle's six main colour versions.

115.5cc is the engine capacity, and the cost of the same is Rs.53,811.

When comparing commuter vehicles to more performance-oriented ones, take into account that mileage is usually better. As a result, clients shopping for the current sports bike really shouldn't anticipate it having the same fuel economy as commuting two-wheelers.
