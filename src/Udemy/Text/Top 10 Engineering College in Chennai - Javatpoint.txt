            Top 10 Engineering College in Chennai Chennai is home to many educational institutes and colleges that provide high-quality education and a wide range of courses based on their interests and preferences. Here, we have listed the Top Engineering Colleges in Chennai, along with other information such as courses that offer, their address, the official website. Eligibility Criteria of Engineering Institutes Located in Chennai The results of the JEE (Main) are used to decide which engineering colleges in Chennai would admit applicants. Candidates who score well in the JEE Main exam get the option to study at the top universities. Tamil Nadu Engineering Admission is an annual entrance exam for students seeking admission to reputed Tamil Nadu colleges and organizations. Apart from this, a formal admission exam is used by a few engineering schools. To appear for the JEE Main, students must complete their 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as their core courses. Candidates must also be 17 years or more. Information on academics In Chennai, there are many engineering institutes where students may learn modern engineering fields as per their interests. Electronics, Communication, Information, Mechanical Engineering, Technology, and Computer Science are just a few of the most popular fields. Textile Technology, Environmental Engineering, Manufacturing, Biomedical Engineering, and Automation are some emerging specialties offered. Job Opportunities Engineering students have a wide range of career opportunities. Engineers are in high demand, owing to the growing requirement for technically skilled individuals. Engineering is used in nearly every business. As a result, the demand for engineering specialists is increasing at an unprecedented rate, as does the compensation package on offer. Mechanical Engineers, Software Engineers, Hardware Engineers, I.T. Engineers, and Marine Engineers are some of the available job opportunities. Top Engineering Colleges in Chennai 1. Indian Institute Of Technology Madras IIT Madras (Indian Institute of Technology Madras) is a public engineering institute in Chennai. The Indian Institute of Technology of Madras (IITM) was established in 1959 as the third institute under the Indian government's Institutes of Technology Act, 1961. The National Institutional Ranking Framework has named the institute the best engineering college in the country for four consecutive years: 2016, 2017, 2018, and 2019.  
            
            
              
              Courses 
              B.Tech, BS + MS, BA + MA, MBA, M.Tech, B.Tech + M.Tech, M.Sc., and Ph.D. 
              
              
              Official Website 
              https://www.iitm.ac.in/ 
              
              
              Address 
              Institute Of Technology, 600036 Chennai, Tamil Nadu 
              
            
            2. Sathyabama Institute Of Science And Technology Dr. Jeppiar created the Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology in 1987. Sathyabama Engineering College was the old name of the institute. In 2001, it was classified as a Christian Minority Educational Institution, giving it the status of a designated university. Mechanical engineering, electronic and communication engineering, chemical engineering, aeronautical engineering, automobile engineering, and electronic and instrumentation engineering are just a few of the degrees offered by the institute. In addition to engineering studies, the university offers undergraduate degrees in architecture, physics, designing, financial accounting, English, commerce, psychology, and dental surgery.  
            
            
              
              Courses 
              B.Tech , MBA, B.Sc., B.Pharm, L.L.B, BA LLB (Hons.), B.Com, BDS, B.Com + LLB (Hons.), M.Tech, M.Sc., BA, B.E 
              
              
              Official Website 
              www.sathyabama.ac.in 
              
              
              Address 
              Chennai, Tamil Nadu 600119, Kamaraj Nagar, Semmancheri, OMR 
              
            
            3. Madras Christian College MCC (Madras Christian College) is a Tamil Nadu-based art and science college. It is one of Asia's oldest existing institutions, having been established in the year 1837. It is a self-governing organization based in Chennai's Tambaram neighborhood and the University of Madras is linked with it. Initially, it was opened as a boys' school and is one of the state's first higher education institutions. Three sections make up the 365-acre property.  
            
            
              
              Courses 
              BBA, B.Com, BA, B.Sc., MCA, BCA, M.Sc., MA, M.Com, BSW, and MSW 
              
              
              Official Website 
              www.mcc.edu.in 
              
              
              Address 
              Tambaram East, Tambaram, Tamil Nadu 600059 
              
            
            4. Easwari Engineering College, Chennai Easwari Engineering College is located in Bharathi Salai, Chennai, and it was founded in 1996-97. It is a self-financing commercial institute formed with the sanction of the Tamil Nadu government and the AICTE, New Delhi. It has been connected with Anna University (Chennai) since 2002, after previously being affiliated with the University of Madras. NAAC has classified it as an A-rated university. It is a division of the Sri Ramaswamy Memorial Group of Higher Education Institutions.  
            
            
              
              Courses 
              B.E. (All branches), B.Tech (IT, CS, etc.), and B.E. Automobile Engineering 
              
              
              Official Website 
              https://srmeaswari.ac.in/ 
              
              
              Address 
              Ramapuram, Tamil Nadu 600089, 162, Bharathi Salai 
              
            
            5. Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT), Chennai Vellore Institute of Technology, popularly known as VIT University, was founded in 1984. It is a Deemed University, acknowledged by the Indian Government's Ministry of Human Resource Management and recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC). The institute has been authorized by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and it has received Grade 'A' certification from the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC). VIT Chennai was founded in the year 2010. The institute's principal goal is to deliver high-quality teaching, learning, and research, setting it apart from other institutions.  
            
            
              
              Courses 
              Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) All Branches 
              
              
              Official Website 
              https://chennai.vit.ac.in/ 
              
              
              Address 
              Rajan Nagar, Chennai, Tamil Nadu 600127 Kelambakkam - Vandalur Rd 
              
            
            6. Hindustan Institute of Technology and Science, Chennai Hindustan Institute of Technology and Science (HITS) was established in 1985. The University Grants Commission granted the institute with the "University Status" under clause 3 of the UGC Act 1956. Later, in the year 2008-09, the Hindustan College of Engineering is renamed to Hindustan Institute of Technology and Science. It is one of India's most prestigious engineering colleges for further education in science and technology. In addition, the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) has given the Institution an "A" rating and the All India Council for Technical Education has authorized it.  
            
            
              
              Courses 
              B.Tech, Integrated B.Tech + M.Tech, and B.Tech + MM 
              
              
              Official Website 
              https://hindustanuniv.ac.in/ 
              
              
              Address

            7. Sri Sairam Engineering College, Chennai MJF.Ln.Leo Muthu, Chairman of Sapthagiri Educational Trust, established Sri Sairam Engineering College in Chennai in 1995 as a non-profit, non-minority institution. Because of a well-defined vision, a thoroughly dedicated purpose, and committed leadership, Sri Sairam Engineering College ranks among the country's best educational institutions. The Institution has developed into a massive combination of gorgeous buildings, state-of-the-art, sophisticated labs, contemporary libraries, and a spectacular sports complex-each has a landmark in its own right-spanning 300 acres.

            8. SRM Institute of Science and Technology - SRM University, Chennai Sri Ramaswami Memorial University (SRM University) is a well-known private institution in India that provides undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctorate programs in various fields. The institute was founded in 1985 as SRM Engineering College, but in 2003-04, it was granted Deemed status, and its name was changed to SRM Institute of Science and Technology. The University Grants Commission granted the institute full university status in 2006 under the UGC Act of 1956.  
            
            
              
              Courses

In 2018, SSN College became a self-contained institute. This Institution provides 21 engineering courses at both undergraduate and postgraduate levels.
